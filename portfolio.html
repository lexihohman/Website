1.2.5 Project- Spongebob vs Patrick Fireball

This game is called Spongebob vs Patrick Fireball. Both characters have three lives and can shoot fireballs at one another. When either character is shot, they lose a life. Eventually, when all lives are gone, that player dies and the character who wins is presented on the screen.
